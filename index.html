Enclose

* For optimal game experience, please resize the screen using cmd(for Macs) or ctrl(for PCs) and +/- to adjust the browser size.


  1. Use your arrow keys to move your avatar.

  2. Your avatar is green when on a "safe-spot" and red when on a "danger-spot".

  3. The border is the only safe spots to begin with. To fill in the board, start from a safe spot, venture out into an unsafe spot, and land back into a safe spot. Doing so will fill the area you've outlined with your avatar.

  4. You can now create further shapes off the area you've filled.

  5. Once you've filled in 70% or more of the area, you will proceed onto the next level.

  6. Every Level you are afforded five lives. The lives reset at every level. The marbles get faster with each level.
  7. And that's it! Enjoy the many shapes that Enclose allows you to create.
